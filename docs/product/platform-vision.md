# Platform Vision

## Product Name (Working)

ClaimsOS

## Vision

ClaimsOS is a Property Intelligence and Claims Operations Platform designed for public adjusters, appraisers, umpires, attorneys, contractors, and insurance dispute professionals.

The platform combines:

* Property Intelligence
* Weather Intelligence
* Claims Management
* Workflow Enforcement
* AI Guidance
* Operational Quality Control

Unlike traditional CRMs, ClaimsOS actively manages claim progression and ensures no file is left behind.

## Core Principle

> Every file must always have a next action.

The platform continuously evaluates:

* Current stage
* Responsible party
* Required documents
* Required communications
* Outstanding tasks
* Compliance requirements
* Deadlines
* Escalation triggers

## Target Market

### Primary

* Public Adjusting Firms

### Secondary

* Insurance Attorneys
* Appraisers
* Umpires
* Roofing Contractors
* Restoration Contractors

## Strategic Differentiators

### Property Intelligence

Property history, permit data, weather events, imagery, and risk analysis.

### Workflow Enforcement

Tasks cannot be completed unless requirements are satisfied.

### Playbooks

Claim-type-specific operational workflows.

### AI Claim Strategy

Provides guidance, identifies missing evidence, and recommends next actions.

### Public Adjusting Business in a Box

New firms can launch using proven workflows, templates, contracts, and processes.

## Long-Term Goal

Become the operating system for property insurance claims professionals.
