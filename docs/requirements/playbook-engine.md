# Playbook Engine Requirements

## Purpose

Provide reusable claim-handling workflows.

## Definition

A Playbook is a predefined operational process.

## Examples

* Florida Wind Claim
* Florida Water Claim
* Hail Claim
* Commercial Roof Claim
* Appraisal Matter
* Umpire Matter

## Playbook Components

### Stages

Define claim progression.

### Tasks

Define required actions.

### Documents

Define required forms.

### Communications

Define required correspondence.

### Deadlines

Define compliance requirements.

### AI Guidance

Provide contextual recommendations.

## Playbook Assignment

A claim may be assigned one or more playbooks.

## Playbook Versioning

Changes must not impact existing claims.

Every playbook must support:

* Draft Version
* Active Version
* Archived Version

## Future Requirement

Marketplace allowing firms to share or license playbooks.
