# Product Vision

## Mission

Provide a unified platform for contractors, public adjusters, attorneys,
and property owners to investigate, manage, and optimize property insurance claims.

## Target Users

- Public Adjusters
- Roofing Contractors
- Restoration Companies
- Insurance Attorneys
- Property Owners

## Core Problem

Current solutions provide fragmented data and workflows.
Users must combine multiple systems for:

- Property data
- Weather reports
- Permit history
- Claim management
- Document analysis

## Product Goal

Create a single platform that turns data into actionable recommendations.

## Strategic Differentiators

### AI Claim Strategy Assistant

Determine:
- Missing evidence
- Claim strengths
- Claim weaknesses
- Recommended next actions

### Property Timeline Engine

Create a complete historical timeline for every property.

### Carrier Intelligence

Track carrier-specific trends and claim outcomes.

### Property Digital Twin

Maintain a continuously updated property history and risk profile.
